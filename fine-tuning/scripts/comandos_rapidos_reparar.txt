==========================================
COMANDOS RÁPIDOS PARA REPARAR ERROR 500
==========================================

PASO 1: Autenticarse (ejecutar en tu máquina Windows)
------------------------------------------------------
gcloud auth login


PASO 2: Conectar a la VM y reparar (copiar todo y pegar)
--------------------------------------------------------
gcloud compute ssh --zone "europe-southwest1-b" "gpt-oss-20b" --project "mamba-001" --command="cd ~/capibara6/backend && pkill -f capibara6_integrated_server && sleep 2 && nohup python3 capibara6_integrated_server.py > ../logs/server.log 2>&1 &"


PASO 3: Verificar que funciona (copiar y pegar)
-------------------------------------------------
gcloud compute ssh --zone "europe-southwest1-b" "gpt-oss-20b" --project "mamba-001" --command="sleep 3 && curl -s http://localhost:5001/health | head -10"


PASO 4: Si hay problemas, ver logs (copiar y pegar)
---------------------------------------------------
gcloud compute ssh --zone "europe-southwest1-b" "gpt-oss-20b" --project "mamba-001" --command="tail -30 ~/capibara6/logs/server.log"
